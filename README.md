# 📊 Customer Credit Risk & Loan Approval Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer data to understand **credit risk** and **loan approval patterns**. The goal is to identify high-risk customers, improve loan decision-making, and uncover key business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze factors affecting loan approval
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Understand customer distribution based on income, experience, and employment
* Provide data-driven insights for better decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Python** → Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* **SQL (PostgreSQL)** → Data Analysis
* **Power BI** → Data Visualization & Dashboard

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

* Age
* Income
* Loan Amount
* Credit Score
* Experience
* Employment Type
* Education
* City
* Loan Approval Status
* Risk Level (Engineered Feature)

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 Loan Approval Analysis

* Approval rate by income, education, employment type, and city
* Identification of high approval segments

### ⚠️ Credit Risk Analysis

* Risk distribution across customers
* Relationship between income, loan amount, and risk
* Credit score impact on loan approval

### 👥 Customer Segmentation

* Segmentation based on income and experience
* Identification of high-value and high-risk groups

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview

* KPI summary (Approval Rate, Risk %, Avg Income, etc.)
* Approval vs Rejection distribution

### 2. Loan Approval Analysis

* Approval patterns across customer segments

### 3. Credit Risk Analysis

* Risk behavior and approval relationship

### 4. Customer Segmentation

* Customer grouping and segment performance

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* High-income and salaried customers have higher approval rates
* Low-income customers are more likely to be high risk
* Credit score strongly influences loan approval decisions
* Experienced customers tend to have better approval chances

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

* Helps banks identify **high-risk applicants**
* Improves **loan approval strategies**
* Supports **data-driven decision-making**

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can be used to **reduce financial risk and improve loan approval efficiency** using real-world analytical techniques.
